About Us


The Washington University Climbing Club is a loose association of students and faculty that enjoy rock climbing and bouldering both indoors and outdoors. We offer discount memberships to the local indoor climbing gyms, and we arrange numberous outdoor climbing trips every semester.

As a member of the club, you will gain access to a group of friendly climbers of all skill levels who are interested in both teaching and learning how to climb harder. The club organizes carpools to the local gyms every week and there are always members willing to go climb and hang out.
